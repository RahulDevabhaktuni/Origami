Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  • Chameleons can change color based on their surroundings.
  • Chameleons are reptiles.
  • Chameleons have a tail used to wrap on branches.

Interesting Facts about the Camel

  • Camels survive really long without water because they store water in their hump.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes.
  • Camel ears are furry.

Interesting Facts about the pigeon.

  • Pigeons are considered to be one of the most intelligent birds on the planet.
  • Pigeons are gray.
  • Pigeons are a subspecies of bird.
